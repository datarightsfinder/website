About Data Rights Finder

Let's be honest. We've all agreed to privacy policies we haven't read or understood. And when we do have an issue we want to resolve, not many of us know what to do.

Organisations should make their privacy policies easy to understand. But not all of them do. To help with this problem, we've analysed organisations' privacy policies to make it clearer how they use data, including data about you.

And we're making it easier to know what your rights are if you want to use any of your data protection rights. We'll guide you through making requests to change how data about you is used.

Currently, we're focussing on covering fintech organisations. We'll be expanding our coverage in the future. Let us know at {{ settings.contact_email }} if there's a specific organisation you'd like to be added to the site
