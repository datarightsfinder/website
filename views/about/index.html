About Data Rights Finder

Organisations should make their privacy policies easy to understand. But not all of them do. To help with this problem, Data Rights Finder contributors have analysed organisations' privacy policies to make it clearer how they use data, including data about you.

Data Rights Finder makes it easier to understand your rights. We'll guide you through making requests to change how data about you is used.

Currently, we're focussing on covering financial services organisations. We'll be expanding our coverage in the future. Let us know at contact@datarightsfinder.org if there's a specific organisation you'd like to be added to the site.
